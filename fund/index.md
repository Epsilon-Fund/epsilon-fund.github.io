## Philosophy

εpsilon is built on the belief that crypto markets, despite their volatility, are increasingly amenable to systematic, data-driven approaches. We combine rigorous quantitative research with macro awareness to develop strategies that are robust across market regimes.

## Approach

Our research process is structured around three pillars: momentum and trend-following strategies, macro overlay analysis, and cross-sectional relative value. Each strategy is developed through a strict research pipeline — hypothesis, backtesting, stress testing, and walk-forward validation before any live consideration.

We treat crypto as a 365-day market with unique structural characteristics — perpetual funding dynamics, on-chain transparency, and liquidity profiles that differ fundamentally from traditional assets. Our edge comes from understanding these nuances deeply.

## Fund I — In Development

εpsilon Fund Alpha is currently in the research and development phase. The team is focused on building institutional-quality infrastructure, validating strategies across full market cycles, and establishing a track record before external capital is considered.

## Team

Three researchers from diverse backgrounds — quantitative development, economics, and machine learning — working systematically toward a common research agenda.
