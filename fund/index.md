<div class="fund-section">
  <h2 class="fund-section-label">PHILOSOPHY</h2>
  <div class="fund-section-body">
    <span class="fund-lead">Crypto markets are inefficient. We intend to exploit that — systematically.</span>
    <p>εpsilon is built on a simple premise: that volatility creates opportunity, and opportunity rewards preparation. We combine rigorous quantitative research with macro awareness to develop strategies that hold up across full market cycles — not just bull runs.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="fund-section">
  <h2 class="fund-section-label">APPROACH</h2>
  <div class="fund-section-body">
    <span class="fund-lead">Every strategy starts with a hypothesis. Every hypothesis earns its place through data.</span>
    <p>Our research process runs on three pillars: momentum and trend-following, macro overlay analysis, and cross-sectional relative value. Each idea moves through a strict pipeline — hypothesis, backtesting, stress testing, walk-forward validation — before it gets anywhere near live consideration.</p>
    <p>We treat crypto as a 365-day market with structural characteristics that differ fundamentally from traditional assets. Perpetual funding dynamics, on-chain transparency, and unique liquidity profiles are features, not noise. Our edge comes from understanding them deeply.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="fund-section">

  <div class="fund-section-body">
    <span class="fund-lead">Building the infrastructure before deploying the capital.</span>
    <p>εpsilon Fund Alpha is currently in research and development. The team is focused on building institutional-quality infrastructure, validating strategies across full market cycles, and establishing a credible track record. External capital will be considered once that foundation is complete.</p>
